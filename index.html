Pack your bags, , you're going to Ketchikan, Alaska!

A beautiful and quiet town nestled in between gorgeous mountains and refreshing Alaskan rivers. Spend your days hiking, taking ferry rides around the river, and exploring the town filled with great local shops and some of the friendliest people you'll ever meet. With plenty of great places to eat and great faces to meet, you're bound to have a blast in this wonderful Alaskan City.
